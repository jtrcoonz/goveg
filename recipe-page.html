Veggie Burger 4

Cuisine

American

Diet

Vegan, vegetarian

Prep time

30 min.

Ingredients

  • Soy meat
  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Avocado
  • Vegan Aioli

Steps

  1. Press soy meat into a burger shaped patty.
  2. Put the patty on a non-stick pan and into the oven at 375 degrees F for 12 minutes.
  3. Cut vegetables and prepare bun with aioli.
  4. Remove patty from oven. Assemble burger.
  5. Enjoy
